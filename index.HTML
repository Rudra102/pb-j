Roti Recipe

Learn how to make Roti easily

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Ghee
  • Oil

    • Instructions

    1. Put 2 cups of flour in a large bowl.
    2. Add a teaspoon of oil into the flour.
    3. Gradually add water into the flour and knead it until its soft but not sticky or dry.
    4. Take the dough and make it into small balls.
    5. Using a rolling pin, roll the dough until its thin and circluar in shape.
    6. Cook the dough on the stove until it looks well cooked, and puffs up.
    7. Let it cool off then enjoy!
